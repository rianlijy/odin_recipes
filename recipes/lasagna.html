Lasagna

Description

Being one of the oldest pastas in the world, lasagna is truly a staple of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 (24 to 25-ounce) jar marinara sauce (3 cups)
  • 12 ounces low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 15 dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 to 16 ounces whole-milk ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 Farenheit.
  2. Brown the beef and onions.
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  4. Begin layering the lasagna.
  5. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour.
  6. Sprinkle with the remaining mozarella and finish baking.
  7. Cool the lasagna for 15 minutes.
  8. Serve to the guests!
