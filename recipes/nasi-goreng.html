Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is one of the most popular dishes in Asia, as the ingredients are easily available while also being easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups day old rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 150g chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp kecap manis
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 red chillis
  • 2 tsp shrimp paste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add chilli and garlic, stir for 10 seconds.
  3. Add onion, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken, cook until it turns white, then add 1 tbsp kecap manis and cook until chicken is mostly cooked through and a bit caramelised.
  5. Add rice, 2 tbsp kecap manis and shrimp paste. Stir constantly, for 2 minutes until sauce reduces down and rice grains start to caramelise.
  6. Serve, garnished with garnishes of choice.
